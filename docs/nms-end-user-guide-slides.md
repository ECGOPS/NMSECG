## Slide 1 — Welcome

ECG Network Management System (NMS)

- Track faults, inspections, and load readings
- Works online and offline; real-time updates

## Slide 2 — Who Should Use This

- Managers, engineers, technicians
- Use daily to capture data, collaborate, and resolve issues

## Slide 3 — Sign In

1) Click Sign In
2) Use your ECG Microsoft (Azure AD) account
3) If access is limited, contact your admin

## Slide 4 — Home & Navigation

- Dashboard: stats, recent activity, filters
- Menu: Faults, Inspections, Load Monitoring, Chat, Broadcasts
- Profile: role, region/district scope

## Slide 5 — Create a Fault

1) Go to Faults → New
2) Enter details (location, type, description)
3) Add photos (optional)
4) Save (works offline if no network)

## Slide 6 — Update/Resolve a Fault

1) Open a fault from the list
2) Add notes/status changes
3) Attach photos or documents
4) Mark Resolved when complete

## Slide 7 — Substation Inspection

1) Inspections → Substation → New
2) Follow checklist; capture GPS & photos
3) Save offline if needed
4) Submit to sync when online

## Slide 8 — Overhead Line Inspection

1) Inspections → Overhead Line
2) Record findings, attach media
3) Save offline; submit later

## Slide 9 — Load Monitoring

1) Load Monitoring → Select asset/date
2) Enter readings
3) Save (offline supported)
4) Review trend charts

## Slide 10 — Offline Use

- App stores drafts locally when offline
- Pending counts show unsynced items
- Data auto-syncs when online returns

## Slide 11 — Sync & Status

- Check sync badge/counter in the header
- Manual Sync button (if available)
- Keep the app open until sync completes

## Slide 12 — Photos & GPS Tips

- Allow camera and location permissions
- Use good lighting and steady shots
- Ensure GPS is enabled for accurate tagging

## Slide 13 — Chat & Broadcasts

- Chat: team discussions per region/district
- Broadcasts: organization-wide announcements
- Requires internet connection

## Slide 14 — Search & Filters

- Use filters for date, region, district, status
- Combine filters to narrow results quickly

## Slide 15 — Notifications & Alerts

- In-app toasts confirm actions
- Review warnings before submitting

## Slide 16 — Common Issues

- Cannot sign in: verify ECG Microsoft account
- Limited access: request role approval from admin
- Sync stuck: check network; try manual sync

## Slide 17 — Best Practices

- Save frequently when on poor networks
- Capture clear photos of critical issues
- Keep device charged for field work

## Slide 18 — Support

- Contact your regional/district admin for access
- Report issues with screenshots and time

## Slide 19 — Quick Demo (2–3 min)

1) Sign in
2) Create a fault
3) Save offline; go online; sync
4) Post a chat message

## Slide 20 — Thank You

- Your data improves reliability and response time
- Feedback welcomed to enhance NMS
